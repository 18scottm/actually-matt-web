Unit 6 - Quality Control

Modules

  • Module I: Validating Websites
  • Module II: Testing Usability
  • Module III: Reflections

    • Quality Control, the most important unit of Web Page Design. This puts your websites to the test, by validating them making sure that it's error free, and testing usability which basically tests the user on how well it is to use your website. The most important thing I learned about Quality Control is that, you dont know how many errors there could be on your website. I had well over 70+ Errors gone undetected. Which kind of baffled me. This is why this unit is so important. The thing about this unit that frustrated me alot was adding all those alt attributes, to the images.
